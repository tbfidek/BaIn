1. Introduction

1.1 Purpose

The purpose of this document is to present a detailed description of the web application BaIn (Baby Info Manager). This application will provide users (families or couples) with a useful tool to monitor and manage the resources needed to care for their children and to keep every memory of their child's life.

1.2 Intended Audience and Reading Suggestions

The users of this application are parents and guardians who need an efficient and intuitive solution to manage the resources needed to care for their children. Those who want to use the application can be of any age and level of knowledge of technology.

1.3 Product Scope

The purpose of the application is to offer users help in terms of managing the resources needed to care for their children, the feeding and sleeping schedule, the medical history, the relationship with other children, and the chronological view (timeline) of the most important moments related to each individual child.

2. Overall Description

2.1 Product Perspective

kiddoCare is an application developed by three students who want to help preserve children's data and memories. They want this application to be a substitute for a diary, a photo album, or a medical portfolio.

2.2 Product Functions

  • Each user will have access to the following features:
    • About us
    • Create an account
    • Login
    • Create your child profile
    • Manage your child profile
    • Manage (view, publish, delete) personal photos
    • Edit parent profile (name, password, profile picture)
    • Gallery
    • Sleeping Schedule
    • Feeding Time
    • Medical History
    • Dark Mode

2.2 User Classes and Characteristics

  • Parents with children: This class of users represents the main users of the application and will use all the functionalities of the application. They will need functionalities such as managing the food schedule, managing the sleep schedule, recording multimedia resources, recording the medical history, relating to other children, and any other information they wish to add.
  • Families or couples of people who take care of children: This class of users represents secondary users who will access the application to have access to the information regarding the children taken care of. They will need functionalities such as access to the food and sleep program and multimedia resources.

2.3 Operating Environment

The developed product can be used on computers and phones with a stable Internet connection.Can be used on any device with a web browser that supports HTML5, CSS and JavaScript.

5. Other Nonfunctional Requirements

5.1 Safety Requirements

The application will ensure the confidentiality of children's health data, personal details, and family members' data. This information will be kept private and protected against unauthorized access.

5.2 Security Requirements

The users accounts are protected by a login system using bcrypt encryption of the passwords and access tokens in order to maintain their privacy and keep their data safe. Other security measures that are taken: prevention of SQL-injection attacks, blocking of multiple consecutive requests coming from a user in order to prevent application slowdown and blocking the users that have been reported for inappropriate photo uploads.
